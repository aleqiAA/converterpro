About ConverterPro

Free tools for file conversion and professional CV building

Our Story

ConverterPro was created by developers in Nairobi, Kenya to solve a simple problem: converting files and building professional CVs should be free, fast, and easy.

We noticed that most file conversion tools either charge hidden fees, sell your data, or are unreliable. We built ConverterPro as an alternative — a completely free, privacy-first platform for developers, job seekers, and professionals worldwide.

What We Do

ConverterPro offers two main services:

📄 Free File Converter

  • Convert PDFs to images, documents, and back
  • Split, merge, compress, and rotate PDFs
  • Delete or extract pages from PDFs
  • Add watermarks and signatures
  • No file size limits (up to 15MB per file)
  • Files deleted immediately after processing — we never store your data

🎯 Professional CV Builder

  • Build a professional CV in minutes
  • ATS score checker to ensure HR systems can read your CV
  • AI-powered cover letter generation
  • Multiple professional templates
  • Free tier available with basic features
  • Download as PDF instantly

Why Trust ConverterPro?

  • 100% Free: Core features are completely free with no hidden charges.
  • Fast & Reliable: Built on modern cloud infrastructure for instant processing.
  • Kenya-Made: Created by African developers, supporting the tech ecosystem in East Africa.
  • Open & Transparent: Our code and policies are open for inspection.

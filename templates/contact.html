Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Is ConverterPro really free?

Yes! All core features are 100% free. We don't charge for file conversions or the CV builder. Premium CV templates cost extra, but the free classic template is fully featured.

🔒 Do you store my files?

No. All files are deleted immediately after conversion. We don't store, track, or analyze your documents. Your privacy is guaranteed.

💳 Why do you need my email/phone?

We only ask for contact info when you download a paid CV template. For free file conversions, no personal info is needed.

🚀 What file types do you support?

We support PDFs, images (PNG, JPG, GIF, WebP), documents (DOCX, XLSX, TXT), and more. Check our converter page for the full list.

📱 Can I use ConverterPro on my phone?

Yes! ConverterPro is fully mobile-responsive. You can convert files and build CVs from any device with a browser.

⏱️ How long does conversion take?

Most conversions complete instantly or within seconds. Large files (10MB+) may take up to 30 seconds.

💡 Can I suggest a feature?

Absolutely! We love feature requests. Email us at support@converterpro.com or send us a DM on Twitter with your idea.

🤝 Are you interested in partnerships?

Yes! If you'd like to integrate ConverterPro into your service or discuss a collaboration, reach out to us at support@converterpro.com.

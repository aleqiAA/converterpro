Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Read how we protect your data.

Last updated: January 2024

🔒 Our Core Promise: We do NOT collect, store, or sell your personal data. All files are deleted immediately after processing. Period.

1. Introduction

ConverterPro ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and services at converterpro.com.

This policy complies with Kenya's Data Protection Act 2019 (DPA) and GDPR-aligned best practices for user privacy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 File Conversion (No Personal Data Collected)

When you use our file converter:

  • We do NOT collect, read, or store your files.
  • We do NOT collect personal information from your files (e.g., name, email, ID numbers in documents).
  • All files are deleted immediately after conversion completes, usually within seconds.
  • We only use your files temporarily in memory to process the conversion.

2.2 CV Builder (Optional Information)

When you use our CV Builder, you may voluntarily provide:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location/city
  • Professional summary and work experience
  • Education details
  • Skills

This information is stored in your browser session only and only transmitted to our servers when you download a paid CV template. If you use the free CV builder, this data stays entirely on your device.

2.3 Payment Information (Stripe/IntaSend)

If you purchase a premium CV template, payment is processed by:

  • Stripe (for international payments)
  • IntaSend (for M-Pesa payments in Kenya)

We do NOT store your credit card information. Payment processors handle all financial data according to their own privacy policies.

2.4 Website Usage Data

We may automatically collect:

  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on our site
  • Referring website

This is collected for analytics purposes only, using Google Analytics. We do NOT track individual users or link this data to personal identity.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information only for:

  • Processing file conversions: To deliver your converted files
  • CV building: To generate your professional PDF
  • Payment processing: To complete premium purchases
  • Website improvements: To understand usage patterns and fix bugs
  • Customer support: To respond to your inquiries (contact form)
  • Legal compliance: To comply with applicable laws

We never:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your files with advertisers or analytics companies
  • Use your CV information for recruitment or hiring purposes
  • Send unsolicited marketing emails (only transactional emails)

4. Cookies

ConverterPro uses minimal cookies:

  • Session cookies: To keep you logged in while building a CV
  • Security cookies (CSRF tokens): To prevent unauthorized requests
  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics (with anonymization enabled)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work properly.

5. Google Ads & AdSense

ConverterPro uses Google AdSense to display ads and generate revenue for free services.

  • Google Analytics and Google Ads may collect browsing data for ad personalization
  • You can opt out of personalized ads in your Google Ads Settings
  • We do NOT pass your file contents or personal CV data to Google

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • HTTPS encryption: All data in transit is encrypted
  • Rate limiting: Protects against DDoS and abuse
  • CSRF protection: Prevents unauthorized requests
  • Input validation: Prevents injection attacks
  • Secure headers: Content Security Policy and X-Frame-Options enabled
  • Immediate file deletion: No long-term storage of user files

7. Your Rights (Data Protection Act 2019)

Under Kenya's Data Protection Act 2019, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request what personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request removal of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Receive your data in a standard format
  • Objection: Opt out of data processing

To exercise these rights, email support@converterpro.com with your request.

8. Children's Privacy

ConverterPro is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete it immediately.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party services (Stripe, Google, Twitter, etc.). We are NOT responsible for their privacy practices. Please read their privacy policies before sharing information with them.

10. Data Retention

  • File conversions: Deleted within seconds of processing
  • CV builder data: Stored only during your session (can be cleared by clearing browser cookies)
  • Contact form submissions: Retained for 90 days for support purposes, then deleted
  • Analytics data: Retained for 26 months (Google Analytics default)
  • Server logs: Retained for 7 days for security purposes

11. International Data Transfers

ConverterPro is based in Kenya. If you access our services from outside Kenya, your data may be transferred to Kenya. By using ConverterPro, you consent to this transfer.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Modified" date. Continued use of ConverterPro constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
